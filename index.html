ÉTÉ

A summer collection with white as the anchor and rose as the primary motif ÉTÉ, joyously celebrates the cross pollination of cultures, a fusion of Tamil & French life. Culture is fluid, forever moving and evolving through borrowing and exchange of ideas, design and aesthetics. Intermingling of ideologies and practices is how newness seeps in. ÉTÉ is our attempt to mindfully capture the mélange of creative fusion around us.

MAKING OF THE LINEN SARI

The making of the linen sari is nothing short of an extraordinary journey of our love for the warp and weft. Back in 2010 the passionate experiment with indigenous textiles and artisanal skill coupled with the desire to modernize and deconstruct the sari space led ‘Anavila’ to innovate ‘the linen sari’, today a must-have in every sari wardrobe.
